This table links each genotype used in the common garden experiments 
to the site where it was collected and site and year where seeds were multiplied.
Contents of each column described below.

genotypeID: unique code assigned to each genotype 
Site.code: Seed collection site	
Plant.ID: Identifies which of the three collected plants was grown out (A, B, or C)	
batch: Year and location of greenhouse seed grow out
